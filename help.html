Help & Support

Important Notice

Once a Day is designed for entertainment and personal reflection only. It is NOT a mental health service, medical service, counseling platform, or substitute for professional care.

If you need mental health support, please contact a qualified mental health professional.

If you are in crisis: Please contact emergency services (911) or call/text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. In other countries, find your local crisis line at findahelpline.com

Getting Started

Welcome to Once a Day! Each day, you'll receive a thought-provoking question to answer and explore what others think.

  • Answer the daily question to share your perspective
  • Read and react to other answers
  • Leave comments to engage in conversations

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my nickname?

Your nickname is set during onboarding and cannot be changed to maintain community integrity.

When do new questions appear?

A new question is posted every day at midnight (your local time).

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three dots on any answer or comment and select "Report" to flag content for review. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

How do I block a user?

Tap the three dots on any answer or comment, then select "Block User". You will no longer see any content from that user. You can manage your blocked users list in Settings > Blocked Users.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile information (email, nickname) will be permanently deleted
  • Your answers and comments will be anonymized but may remain visible
  • This action cannot be undone

Account deletion is processed immediately.
